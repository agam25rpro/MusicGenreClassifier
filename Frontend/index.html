HOW IT WORKS

01. Chunking

Instead of processing the entire song at once, the audio is mathematically split into overlapping 4-second chunks. This ensures continuous acoustic patterns are captured smoothly.

02. Mel Spectrograms

The raw audio waveforms are algorithmically converted into 2D visual representations called Mel Spectrograms. This maps frequency and amplitude over time, turning sound into an image.

03. Neural Network

These generated images are passed through a TensorFlow Convolutional Neural Network (CNN). The AI uses spatial feature detection to identify textures in the sound mathematically.

04. Majority Voting

Every single 4-second chunk gets its own independent prediction array. The final genre output is statistically calculated using a majority vote across the entire duration of the track.
